The Magic of the
Hook & Yarn

Crochet is a celebration of color and patience. Originating from the French word "croche", this craft turns vibrant spools of yarn into wearable, huggable art.

At Crochetbykomiko, we believe the world needs more brightness. Every loop and knot is crafted by hand, ensuring that no two pieces are exactly alike. It's slow fashion with a loud, joyful heartbeat.

Meet Komal Kerur

Founder & Lead Artist

Hello! I'm Komal, the colorful mind behind Crochetbykomiko. What started as a quiet hobby quickly bloomed into a loud, proud passion for creating wearable, joyful art.

In a world of mass production, I find joy in the slow process of crafting by hand. My designs are inspired by sunny days, bright blooms, and the simple desire to make people smile when they wear my creations.
