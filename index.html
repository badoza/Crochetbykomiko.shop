The Art of the
Hook & Yarn

Crochet is a labor of pure love. Originating from the French word "croche", this timeless craft transforms vibrant, flowing threads into breathtaking wearable art.

At Crochetbykomiko, we are obsessed with color and texture. We believe everyday items should make your heart skip a beat. We don't use machines; we use patience, creativity, and a little bit of magic.

"Every stitch is a tiny drop of happiness crafted into existence."

Meet Komal Kerur

Founder & Lead Artist

Hello! I'm Komal, the cheerful mind behind Crochetbykomiko. What started as a quiet hobby quickly bloomed into a loud, proud passion for creating wearable, joyful art.

In a world of mass production, I find pure joy in the slow process of crafting by hand. My designs are inspired by beautiful sunsets, blooming flowers, and the simple desire to make you smile when you hold my creations.
