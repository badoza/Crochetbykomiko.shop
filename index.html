The Magic of the Hook & Yarn

Crochet is more than just a craft; it is an ancient rhythm, a meditation passed down through generations. Originating from the French word "croche" meaning hook, this art form transforms simple threads into intricate, breathable, and timeless masterpieces.

At Crochetbykomiko, we don't just make products; we sculpt yarn. We honor the heritage of this slow-fashion art by bringing a joyful, modern twist to classic techniques. It takes hours of patience, love, and creative vision to bring each knot to life.

"Every single stitch is a tiny hug crafted into existence."

Meet Komal Kerur

Founder & Lead Artist

Hello! I'm Komal, the hands and heart behind Crochetbykomiko. What started as a quiet hobby quickly bloomed into a passionate pursuit of creating wearable and functional art.

I believe that in our fast-paced, machine-made world, there is profound beauty in things made slowly by hand. My designs are inspired by nature, joy, and the elegant simplicity of everyday life. Thank you for supporting independent artistry!
