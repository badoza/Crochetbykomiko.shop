The Magic of the
Hook & Yarn

Crochet is more than just a craft; it is an ancient rhythm. Originating from the French word "croche" (meaning hook), this beautiful art form transforms simple, vibrant threads into intricate, breathable masterpieces.

At Crochetbykomiko, we don't just make products; we sculpt joy. We honor the heritage of slow-fashion by bringing a modern, colorful twist to classic techniques. It takes hours of patience, love, and creative vision to bring each knot to life.

"Every single stitch is a tiny, colorful hug crafted into existence."

Meet Komal Kerur

Founder & Lead Artist

Hello! I'm Komal, the hands and heart behind Crochetbykomiko. What started as a quiet hobby quickly bloomed into a passionate pursuit of creating wearable, colorful, and functional art.

I believe that in our fast-paced, machine-made world, there is profound beauty in things made slowly by hand. My designs are inspired by nature, bright colors, and the simple joy of creating.
